Location

Discover
the Region

Cove Garden sits at the heart of North Cyprus's most beautiful coastal corridor — where history, nature, and Mediterranean life converge.

~ 25 km West

Girne (Kyrenia)

One of the Mediterranean's most enchanting harbour towns. Its medieval castle, crescent-shaped old harbour, and clifftop restaurants make Girne the beating heart of North Cyprus culture and nightlife.

  • Kyrenia Castle & Shipwreck Museum
  • Old Venetian Harbour
  • Bellapais Abbey (12th century)
  • Boutique shops & waterfront dining
Your Home Base

Esentepe

A peaceful village draped in olive groves and citrus trees, facing the open sea. Here, life moves to a slower rhythm. Cove Garden is nestled in this village — steps from the sea and the championship Korineum Golf Course, with Golden Beach on the Karpaz Peninsula within easy reach.

  • Golden Beach · Karpaz Peninsula
  • Korineum Golf & Beach Resort
  • Traditional village atmosphere
  • Crystal-clear snorkelling waters
~ 5 km East

Bahçeli

Nestled at the foot of the Besparmak Mountains, Bahçeli is a tranquil village with sweeping views across the sea to Turkey's distant coastline. The perfect base for mountain hikes and sunset watching.

  • Panoramic sea & mountain views
  • Besparmak (Five Finger) Mountains
  • Peaceful village taverns
  • Unspoiled coastal walking paths
Experiences

What Awaits
You Here

North Cyprus is an island of extraordinary contrasts — ancient and modern, rugged and serene, wild and welcoming.

Beaches & Coast

Swim in water so clear you can count the pebbles below. The North Cyprus coastline remains one of the Mediterranean's best-kept secrets — uncrowded, pristine, and breathtakingly blue.

Golden Beach · Karpaz Snorkelling Sailing from Girne

Golf at Korineum

Play one of the finest golf courses in the Eastern Mediterranean. Korineum Golf & Beach Resort combines championship-standard greens with stunning sea and mountain backdrops.

18-Hole Course Sea Views Club Rental Available

Besparmak Mountains

The Five Finger Mountains tower dramatically above the coast. Hike through pine forests, encounter rare wildlife, and enjoy panoramic views stretching from Cyprus to Turkey from the ridge trails.

Hiking Trails Birdwatching Panoramic Views

Ancient History

Step into a living history book. St. Hilarion Castle perches on a clifftop like something from a fairy tale. Bellapais Abbey stands in Gothic perfection. Kyrenia Castle guards a harbour unchanged for centuries.

St. Hilarion Castle Bellapais Abbey Kyrenia Castle

Girne Harbour Life

Spend an evening at the legendary old harbour of Kyrenia — fishing boats bob at anchor, restaurants spill out onto the quay, and the castle walls glow amber in the lamplight. Pure Mediterranean magic.

Boat Trips Waterfront Dining Sunset Watching

Cypriot Cuisine

Discover the rich flavours of Turkish Cypriot meze — shared plates of halloumi, olives, fresh seafood, stuffed vine leaves, and slow-cooked meats, washed down with local wine or the legendary Cypriot spirit, zivania.

Meze & Mezze Fresh Seafood Local Wines
Day Trip Highlight

Karpaz Peninsula & the Wild Donkeys

Drive to the untamed tip of Cyprus — the Karpaz (Karpas) Peninsula, a UNESCO-protected strip of wild land reaching into the sea between Turkey and Syria. Here, herds of wild donkeys roam freely along golden beaches and ancient ruins, utterly indifferent to the 21st century. A day trip like no other.

Wild Donkeys Golden Sand Beaches Apostolos Andreas Monastery ~1.5h Drive

Cafe Paris

One of the coast's most beloved spots — Cafe Paris combines a sun-drenched terrace with sweeping sea views, freshly baked pastries, iced coffees, and homemade cakes. A short drive from the apartment, it's the perfect breakfast stop or afternoon retreat.

Iced Coffee & Tea Homemade Pastries Sea View Terrace Nearby
